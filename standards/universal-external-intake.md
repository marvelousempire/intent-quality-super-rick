# Universal External-Intake and Autonomous Delivery Law

**Rule:** `RL-SR-INTAKE-001` · **Status:** binding policy; runtime promotion remains receipt-gated

Every external signal supplied to Nephew—repository, social post, media, article, pasted text,
model, product, software, hardware, or comparison question—is a potential Family Office improvement.
It must not receive a summary-only response when a system decision is implied.

## Mandatory flow

`resolve claims → classify every Standard Stack → deeply inspect owners → inventory current family truth → compare → disposition → deliver safely → physically prove → update standards → monitor`

The full standards registry is classified on every intake. Only relevant owners receive deep
inspection. The evidence must include current software, hardware seats, models, contracts, receipts,
known gaps, sovereignty, privacy, license, resource cost, maintenance drag, and human benefit.

## Dispositions

Exactly one primary disposition is required:

- `already-covered` — current capability meets the intent; do not add duplication.
- `optimize-existing` — improve the family-controlled path instead of installing another stack.
- `adapt-pattern` — borrow a bounded pattern while preserving ownership and tenancy.
- `install-integrate` — the external capability has a proven advantage worth its drag.
- `benchmark-candidate` — evidence is insufficient; measure on named family hardware.
- `reject` — benefit does not justify duplication, risk, license, egress, or maintenance.

## Hybrid autonomy

Candidate mode permits reversible local analysis, branches, tests, builds, benchmarks, sandboxed
integration, and receipts. After physical acceptance, a scoped expiring commission may authorize
protected ships, family-controlled deploys/restarts, canaries, rollback, standards updates, and agent
coordination. Purchases, subscriptions, credentials, secrets, public publishing, communication with
people, destructive data operations, privilege escalation, surveillance expansion, legal acceptance,
and unbounded external effects always require approval.

Policy drift, source/license change, failed canary, expired receipt, privacy failure, or rollback
failure contracts autonomy to candidate mode immediately.

## Truth and delivery

Truth stages are `assessed`, `selected`, `implemented`, `physically-proven`, `commissioned`, and
`live`. Only the final three may be described as working. Safe implementation remains required when
work is selected; chat-only analysis is not delivery. Every consequential assessment updates the
owning stack's radar, Why Guide, ledger, alternatives, and signed receipt.
